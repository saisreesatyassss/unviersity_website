Placement at VIT-AP

At VIT-AP University, we are committed to providing our students with excellent placement opportunities. Our dedicated placement cell works tirelessly to connect students with top-tier companies and organizations across various industries. The university has achieved an impressive placement record, with many students securing lucrative job offers and internships.

Our placement team conducts regular training sessions and workshops to enhance students' technical and soft skills, ensuring they are well-prepared for the job market. We also invite industry experts to interact with students and offer insights into industry trends and requirements.

We take pride in the diverse range of companies that visit our campus for placements, offering positions in various domains such as IT, finance, consulting, engineering, marketing, and more. Our students have gone on to work in renowned companies both in India and abroad.

Average Package Offered

Our graduates have received impressive placement packages, with the average package ranging from ₹8 to ₹10 lakhs per annum.

Internship Opportunities

Apart from excellent job placements, we also provide students with exciting internship opportunities at esteemed companies. These internships allow students to gain valuable industry experience and build networks that can boost their career prospects.
